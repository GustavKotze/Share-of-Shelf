About Share of Shelf Calculator

This tool helps you to quickly analyze how much shelf space your brand occupies compared to the total. This informs you about your product's visibility and market presence, helping you strategize for better placement or expansion.

How to use this tool

Physically count the total product facings on the shelf. Then count your brand's number of facings. If you have a target % you wish to achieve, add it to the calculator to see how far from target the number of units on the shelf are.
